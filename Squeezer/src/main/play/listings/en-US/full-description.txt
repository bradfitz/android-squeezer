Remote control for your Logitech Media Server ("Squeezeserver" etc) and players.

Features include:

&bull; Browse the library by album artist, all artists, composers, album, genre, year, playlist, favorites, or new music
&bull; Playlist control (now playing, shuffle, repeat, save/clear/modify playlists)
&bull; Manage players, and groups of players
&bull; Full library search
&bull; Internet radio support (browse, staff picks, search)
&bull; Browse Squeezeserver music folders
&bull; Supports Plugins/Apps
&bull; Library Views and Remote Music Libraries
&bull; Download of local music; track, album, artist, genre, year, playlist and music folder
&bull; Automatic discovery of local servers
&bull; Support for password protected servers
&bull; Connect to mysqueezebox.com
&bull; Control playback from your Android Wear device
&bull; Home Screen Widgets
&bull; Alarm Clock management
&bull; Add/remove music from favorites
&bull; Display lyrics

Squeezer is free, and open source.
